STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOP) - NETWORK MITIGATION
========================================================
1. BROADCAST STORM DETECTED:
   - Identify the source MAC/IP from network_alerts.log.
   - Access the core switch interface via SSH and isolate the affected VLAN/subnet port.
   
2. NON-COMPLIANT WORKSTATION DETECTED:
   - Flag terminals missing licensed OS or updated endpoint safety shields.
   - Revoke domain connection privileges in Active Directory until patches are pushed.
